Version 3.10.0
<br/>
<br/>
<br/>Major
<br/>
<br/>Garrison rework
<br/>- Added police stations: Ransack them for loot and intel, and destroy them for a support boost
<br/>- Rebels will now automatically flip flags once they've overpowered the enemy presence on a marker
<br/>- Improved persistence across the board; cities, garrison unit counts, airfield vics are all persistent
<br/>Added support for JSON saves and save importing / exporting
<br/>Added map Mehland
<br/>City / HR rework
<br/>- Flipping cities is now more dependent on doing tasks instead of killing enemies
<br/>- Minor city missions spawn, little things that are useful to flip towns
<br/>- To permanently flip a city, you must fight a battle for it as a mission when support is high enough
<br/>Rebalances to vehicles and equipment
<br/>- Rebalanced prices of vehicles and statics
<br/>- Rebalanced enemy attack helicopter frequency, group compositions, and response speed
<br/>- Changed number of starting RPGs and added a setting to block unlocking unguided AT
<br/>Antistasi maps and information can be accessed directly from the main menu
<br/>
<br/>
<br/>Minor

<br/>Improved loadout randomization
<br/>Changed some config values to better balance IFA3 factions

<br/>Ammo cargo is now tracked and persistent in the garage with ACE
<br/>Garrison rework - Minor
<br/>- Players can now garrison armed vics for AI to use
<br/>- Garrisons will wake up quicker and be more willing to react to threats
<br/>- PATCOM mortars appear in more places, and are rebalanced to fire more counterbattery and less at infantry
<br/>- HQ radius increased to 75 meters
<br/>- Buildings always persist near rebel markers regardless of HQ distance
<br/>- AI will duck down to avoid incoming mortar fire

<br/>New menu for role selection, use from new battle menu
<br/>- Commander perks are now granted to the acting commander
<br/>- Role counts are now capped dynamically by player count
<br/>- Minor adjustments to role perks
<br/>Minor builder updates
<br/>- Decreased time to build everywhere, decreased further near HQ
<br/>- Buildings near HQ now make it more overt to enemies
<br/>- Added a functional buildable helipad
<br/>City/HR rework - HR income is rebalanced and player spawns eat into HR
<br/>Added a parameter to block garage access for a certain period after flip
<br/>Shift/Control keys now work to give yourself multiple items at once in personal arsenal
<br/>Minor rework to fast travel mechanics
<br/>Added a menu on the in-game escape menu for client settings. More will be added here with future updates
<br/>Added a menu to send remarks from in-game (community servers)
<br/>Additional New Battle Menu updates
<br/>- Added a context menu that provides actions depending on where you are and what you're looking at
<br/>- Currently has actions while looking at vehicles, at HQ, and looking at construction
<br/>- With this, you can change your role, access the vehicle arsenal, and toggle the builder screen clutter
<br/>- Added buttons for common actions to Commander and Admin tabs
<br/>- Replaced the Old Battle Menu button with a war status tab, you can view some previously invisible intel through here
<br/>Counterattacks are now managed by garrisons
<br/>The default bind to toggle the top bar is moved to Ctrl + F12. The hints can be toggled in the options menu
<br/>Boats and aircraft can be placed further away from the garage now (150m instead of 25m)
<br/>Added extra options for the weapon unlock number setting
<br/>Air QRF and attack vehicles face the direction of travel when they spawn in
